The Corona-game (#TheUnfairGame)

The objective of the game

The objective of this game is to be the first player to get to square 64. On the way to the last square you will have to get past several obstacles. Can you get through the game without geting the corona virus? Or maybe you will secure yourself a vaccine and run right past.

What you need

  • 2+ Players
  • The board
  • 3 Dice (6 sided)
  • 1 Game piece per player

Setting up the game

In this game you will plas as someone living in either Africa, Asia or Europe. To fond out who you start as you have to throw 1 dice. If you get 1-2, you are from Africa. If you get 3-4, you are from Asia. During the game players who get Africa are only allowed to use one die, players who get Asia can use 2 dice, and the players who get Europe can use all 3 dice. This rule aplies to all parts of the game. All players can now move their game piece to square 1.

Rules of the game

The youngest player will start the round. To start their turn the player needs to throw the die/dice and move as many squares as the die/dice shows. If a player lands one a square with vaccine, the player needs to throw 8 or more in order to secure the vaccine. This means thats the players who got Africa can't get the vaccine. Any player who secured the vaccine can now land on the squares with the virus without falling down. Players without the vaccine who lands on the COVID squares has to move their game piece to the square pointed to by the arrow. First player to (or past) square 64 wins.
